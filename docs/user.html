User Guide

ROE/OOE Assessment - Guide for Survey Participants

Developer Docs Instructions

Overview

The ROE/OOE Assessment is an organizational effectiveness survey that measures how well your organization supports results-oriented work practices. The survey takes approximately 15-30 minutes to complete depending on the number of questions assigned.

Getting Started

Accessing the Survey

  1. You will receive a unique link via email from your organization
  2. Click the link to open the survey in your web browser
  3. The survey works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices

Supported Browsers

Chrome (recommended)
Firefox
Edge
Safari

Taking the Survey

1

Validation

When you open the survey link, the system will automatically validate your invitation. You will see a success message if your link is valid, or an error message if there's an issue.

2

Personal Information

Depending on your organization's configuration, you may be asked to provide information such as:

  • First Name / Last Name
  • Company / Position / Department
  • Age / Years in Company / Gender
  • City / Country
  • Email / Phone

Note: Only fields required by your organization will be displayed.

3

Survey Instructions

Before starting, you can view the survey instructions by clicking the help icon. The instructions explain the purpose of the survey and how to answer questions.

4

Answering Questions

Each question presents a statement about your organization. For each statement:

  1. Read the statement carefully
  2. Consider how accurately it describes your organization
  3. Click Yes if the statement accurately describes your organization
  4. Click No if the statement does not accurately describe your organization
5

Submission

After answering all questions:

  1. Review your progress on the summary screen
  2. Click "Submit" to send your responses
  3. Wait for confirmation that your submission was successful

Features

Auto-Save: Your progress is automatically saved as you complete the survey. If you close your browser, lose connection, or need a break, simply return to the same link to continue.
Resume Progress: When returning to an incomplete survey, the system detects your saved progress and lets you continue where you left off. Your question randomization is preserved.
Question Randomization: Questions are presented in a random order unique to your session. This randomization is preserved if you resume later.

Troubleshooting

"Invalid Test Link" Error
  • Verify you're using the exact link provided to you
  • Check that the link hasn't expired
  • Contact your administrator for a new link
"Test Already Completed" Message
  • This survey has already been submitted
  • Each link can only be used once
  • Contact your administrator if you need to retake the survey
Survey Won't Load
  • Check your internet connection
  • Try refreshing the page
  • Clear your browser cache
  • Try a different browser
Can't Submit Results
  • Verify your internet connection
  • Try clicking submit again
  • Your results are saved locally if issues persist
  • Contact your administrator for assistance

Privacy & Confidentiality

  • Your individual responses are confidential
  • Results are typically reported in aggregate
  • Personal information is used only for survey administration
  • Data is transmitted securely via HTTPS

Languages Available

  • Spanish: ROE - Resultados Orientados al Entorno
  • English: OOE - Output Orientation Survey

Contact

If you experience technical issues or have questions about the survey, please contact your organization's administrator or HR department.
